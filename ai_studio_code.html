About Pakistan Prize Box

Pakistan Prize Box is a premier knowledge-based platform designed specifically for the people of Pakistan. Our mission is to promote learning and general knowledge through an engaging and rewarding experience.


Users can participate in daily quizzes covering topics from Islamic history to Science and Sports. By answering correctly, users earn points that can be used to unlock virtual prize boxes containing real rewards.


We are 100% free to use. We do not support or host any form of gambling. All rewards are funded through our advertising partners.
